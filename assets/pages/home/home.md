# ITALIAN RECORD LABEL AND MUSIC PUBLISHER

Italian record label and music publisher founded in 2013 and based in Rome, Italy. Sonor Music Editions specializes in rare and obscure sounds from the Italian golden age of production music and soundtracks, that spans from the 1960s to the 1980s, and with a nod to contemporary emerging artists following in those footsteps.
